Why Choose Us?

Information for Investors

1

Problem

The uncertainty of availability, proximity, price, and tender-type acceptance puts a huge burden on motorists when trying to find parking. In fact, motorists devote seventeen hours per year looking for parking spaces. Motorists take a huge gamble by not knowing if parking will be available at school, work, or at an event. Even if spots are available, the proximity of parking to an event, office, or classroom can hamper your efficiency and promptness. Prices can increase with an event taking place or not seem economically feasible for long-term or habitual parking. In a world driven by electronic payment methods, hosts forgo the ability to accept credit cards because of costly card processing fees subsequently losing out on revenue from motorists that do not carry cash.

Additionally, owners of recreational vehicles (RV), boats, and trailers often run into the issue of affordable, available storage. When an owner’s property cannot accommodate a sizeable RV, boat, or trailer the ability to even own one is hampered. Someone may have copious amounts of property with an intention to make passive income with no platform no bridge the property owner with a prospective customer.

2

Solution

A mobile application that provides on-demand parking through the utilization of commercial, industrial, and residential property. A platform that establishes communication and awareness between property owners, motorist, and owners of recreational vehicles and trailers. Deliverance of certainty and peace of mind, effectively eliminating the hassle of parking when attending an event, classroom, or workplace by providing spot reservations. Ultimately, it has the versatility that tends to the needs of parking and storage through one application for different types of users.

3

Our Value Proposition

● Negotiated pricing makes parking affordable.
● Passive income with little effort from the host.
● Parking made simple, flexible, and efficient.
● Peace of mind through availability and certainty of parking.
● Motorists save money and time when parking.
● Payment is completely cashless and automatic. 

4

Customer Segments

The Motal application will reach users on Android and iOS through the Apple App store and the Google Play Store. The customer segments we aim to accomodate are motorists and property owners. Motal will not only appeal to drivers in highly-congested, urban areas with limited existing parking or unaffordable existing parking but to all motorist looking to find parking in all settings, whether rural or suburban. Our services will not only pertain to commuters and visitors but also to RV/boat/trailer owners that have limited options for parking. This will appeal to customers that lack the space to park their RV/boats/trailers but have the desire to own said motorized vehicles without costly storage fees.

Motal also aims to appeal to people willing to make use of empty space on their property. Business owners or homeowners will enjoy passive income with little effort on their part. People rarely have all of their property full with no space availability. Motal aims to get people to host their property/spots that are in areas of high-demand (ex. colleges, stadiums, urban areas).
