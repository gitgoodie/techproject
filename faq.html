FAQ

You've got questions. We've got answers.

1

What is Motal?

Motal is a parking app that connects Motorists in need of a spot with Motalists (that's what we call owners who rent out their spaces). Think of us like the Missing Connections of private parking.

2

How do I use Motal?

You can start using Motal by downloading the app--available both on iPhone and Android. 

3

What if I want to sell my space?

Awesome! Download Motal on your phone to get started and click  "sign up." The app will guide from there but if you're ever lost, feel free to shoot us a line at support@motal.com or click live chat during business hours to speak with a real human being.   

4

How much does it cost?

Prices for each spot are set by the Motalist who owns it and are subject to flucutation depending on regional demand.

5

Who is eligible to rent out a parking space?

Any private parking you legally own is generally eligible to be listed. Certain restrictions may apply, contact support@motal.com for more details.

6

Is it safe?

For safety and legal purposes, we require identification from both Motorists and Motalists. Both Motorists and Motalists are vetted prior to approval.

7

How are users verified?

We use license plate recognition, require a photo of the driver's license, and use a second party background check.

8

Will I meet the Motalist or Motorist?

You certainly might and we encourage it.

9

Wait a minute, I still have questions!

No worries, check out our extended FAQ here or contact support@motal.com for additional inquiries.
